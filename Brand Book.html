Quiet authority. Closer to Wallpaper* than to a pitch deck. Short sentences. Declarative. No exclamation points. No emoji. We do not sell — we describe.

Chapter IV · Type

One serif. One sans, two widths.

A disciplined system. Cormorant Garamond carries the editorial voice. Barlow and Barlow Condensed — same skeleton, two widths — handle everything else in perfect optical harmony.

Cormorant Garamond
Editorial display

Hero headlines, statistics, italic accents. Light weight, tight tracking, generous leading.

Aa Bb Light 300 · Italic 400 · –2% tracking
Barlow
Body & long-form

Proportional sans for paragraphs, leads, captions, form text. Calm, readable, modern.

The brand speaks the way a well-shot film opens — slow, deliberate, lit from behind. Type is the cadence; the words are the breath between the frames.

Light 300 · 22 / 34 · max 48ch
Barlow Condensed
UI · Labels · Eyebrows

Tracked, uppercased, semibold. Vertical density without losing the sans‑serif family relationship.

Chapter V · Mood

Low-key light. Long pacing.

Imagery is moody, cinematic, dark. Cool-to-neutral grade with a warm gold highlight where appropriate. Film grain is signature. Motion is slow, deliberate, ease‑in‑out — never bouncy, never spring.
